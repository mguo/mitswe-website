High School Outreach - Careers in STEM Workshop

Event Details

What: High school students are given the exciting opportunity to explore a diverse number of science, math, and engineering fields. The students will have the opportunity to explore different STEM fields while interacting with current MIT undergraduates. During this event high school students can network with MIT students in a casual environment and talk and ask questions about choosing a major, research and internship experiences, and college in general.
Date: October 3, 2015
Time: 11:00am - 2:00pm
Where: MIT Stratton Student Center, Fourth Floor, Room 407
