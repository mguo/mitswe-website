High School Outreach - SWE High School Project Grants

What are the SWE High School Project Grants?

High School students interested in pursuing an engineering project, but do not have the money to buy supplies are welcome to apply for a SWE High School Project Grants. Interested students must submit a minimum of 300 word essay (maximum 500 words). Maximum grant amount is $200 and students will submit a proposed budget. Students can only apply once per semester. Students should describe their interest in science and technology, a detailed plan of their project, and a statement of what they will accomplish and learn through this project. Project ideas can range from small to large!


Registration for SWE High School Project Grants is now open and ends December 5, 2015.
