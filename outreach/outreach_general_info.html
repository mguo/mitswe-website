FAQs

Why Join National SWE?

National SWE also offers additional benefits to members, including:

  • Access to a huge network of SWE professional members for networking or advice
  • Access to a job and resume database with over 18,000 postings
  • Access to over $500,000 in scholarships
  • Faculty/student socials and networking events
  • Recruiter/student networking events
  • Company plant tours
  • Professional and personal development workshops
  • Member-only seminars, webinars, and speaker events
  • Social events with other SWE Collegiate Sections
  • Mentoring and networking events with SWE Professional Sections
  • Support systems - peer groups, mentors, advisors, industry leaders
  • Section programs including speaker events, seminars, and leadership opportunities
  • SWE Magazine subscription
  • SWE Career Center for internship and full-time employment search and career planning
  • SWE National and Regional Conferences
  • Community events, leadership opportunities, SWE competitions, and more!

What Does National Membership Cost?

MIT SWE will pay for 50% of the national SWE membership fee. Membership options are: 1 year membership | Pay upfront: $20 | Cost to you: $10 5 year membership | Pay upfront: $50 | Cost to you: $25 (5 years = [4 year or until graduation] + 1 year professional)

How Does One Sign Up?

  1. Click here to go to the national SWE website. Click "Join" under the "Membership" tab.
  2. Make sure to register for the MIT (F059) section and use your MIT email address.
  3. Notify MIT SWE of your new membership using this form or you will not be added to the members email list! Please forward your membership confirmation to both.
    4. swe-vp-membership@mit.edu and swe-treasurer@mit.edu to ensure you will be reimbursed.

If you do not have direct deposit set up with MIT, any payment MIT makes to you will be mailed to your PERMANENT (home/family) address and this can be a hassle.
